Platform

Priorities for Mill Bay Malahat (Area A)

The Director's primary job is to be a fierce advocate at the CVRD for the people of Mill Bay/Malahat. I am committed to ensuring the CVRD works for residents, not the other way around.

💰

Rein In Spending

Property taxes have increased 40% since the current Director took office. I will fight out-of-control spending, demand accountability, and restore fiscal responsibility to the CVRD.

🌲

Wildfire & Watershed Resilience

Living on the Malahat Mountain, I understand first-hand the importance of proactive wildfire preparedness and protecting our watershed for long-term community safety.

🏠

Seniors Housing

I am already facilitating a 52-unit affordable housing project for seniors in Mill Bay. He will champion expanded housing options and services for seniors across Mill Bay/Malahat.

🚌

Transportation

Improved transportation connections are essential for a growing community. I will advocate for bus stops for underserved communities such as the Spectacle Lake Mobile Home Park.

🔍

Transparency & Accountability

I will hold the CVRD accountable with full transparency — asking the hard questions and demanding clear answers on behalf of every Mill Bay/Malahat resident.

🛠️

Small Business & Jobs

As an experienced entrepreneur, I will cut red tape to create opportunities for small businesses to ensure that our kids and young families have good local jobs here in Mill Bay/Malahat.

The Numbers Don't Lie

Taxes Are Out of Control

40% Property Tax Increase Under Current Director

Since the current Director took office, average property taxes in Mill Bay have skyrocketed from roughly $3,950 in 2021 to nearly $6,000 in 2024. That's an unacceptable burden on Mill Bay/Malahat families.

I bring the fiscal discipline and accountability needed to reverse this trend and fight for every dollar of taxpayer money.

About Shane

Rooted in
Mill Bay/Malahat

Shane Ryan, 49, is a Malahat resident, small business owner, and former Alternate Director who has spent years fighting for the people of Mill Bay/Malahat. He is announcing his candidacy for Director in the fall 2026 election.

Shane, his wife Christine, and their 11-year-old daughter Elizabeth live entirely off-grid on the Malahat Mountain within the Mill Bay/Malahat riding. Both Shane and Christine are self-employed and deeply committed to this community.

✦

Experienced entrepreneur has built and sold successful businesses, career in private sector finance, and currently operates a successful software consulting company.

✦

Mill Bay Community League President for 5 years fundraising for the local food bank, school lunch programs, and affordable housing.

✦

Active foster family 30+ dogs rescued and fostered.

✦

Values parishioners of St. Francis Xavier Church, Mill Bay.

✦

Christine Ryan, volunteer charity auctioneer, has raised tens of thousands of dollars for many important local causes alongside daughter Elizabeth.

Family & Community

Invested in
Our Community

Shane Ryan has been President of the Mill Bay Community League for over 5 years. During Shane’s tenure as Mill Bay Community League President, Shane has helped raise thousands of dollars for our local CMS foodbank, the lunch program at Francis Kelsey, and is currently facilitating 52 units of affordable seniors housing on Mill Bay Community League land.

Shane Ryan’s wife Christine through her volunteer work has raised significant amounts of money for many important local causes and their daughter Elizabeth is already contributing widely in community events.
