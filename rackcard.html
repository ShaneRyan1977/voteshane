About Shane
Shane Ryan, 49, is a Malahat resident, small business owner, and former CVRD Alternate Director. He lives entirely off-grid in a solar-powered home with his wife Christine and daughter Elizabeth. An experienced entrepreneur previously in private-sector finance, Shane now runs a successful software consulting company. He is a current parishioner of St. Francis Xavier Church, Mill Bay, and an active foster family who has rescued 30+ dogs.
Proven Track Record
Saved 50+ Jobs Fought to protect employment at Western Stevedoring as Alternate Director.
Opposed Director Pay Hikes During COVID Voted against CVRD pay increases, putting residents first.
52 Affordable Senior Housing Units Currently facilitating project in Mill Bay Village with non-profit partner.
5 Years as Community League President Raised thousands for CMS Foodbank & Francis Kelsey lunch program.
Consistent CVRD Accountability Challenged expenditures and demanded justification at every turn.
