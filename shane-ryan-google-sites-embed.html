Platform

Priorities for Area A

The Director's primary job is to be a fierce advocate at the CVRD for the people of Area A. Shane Ryan is committed to ensuring the CVRD works for residents — not the other way around.

💰

Rein In Spending

Property taxes have increased 40% since the current director took office. Shane will stop out-of-control spending, demand accountability, and restore fiscal responsibility to the CVRD.

🌲

Wildfire & Watershed Resilience

Living on the Malahat Mountain, Shane understands first-hand the importance of proactive wildfire preparedness and protecting our watershed for long-term community safety.

🏠

Seniors Housing

Shane is already facilitating a 52-unit affordable housing project for seniors in Mill Bay. He will champion expanded housing options and services for seniors across Area A.

🚌

Transportation

Improved transportation connections are essential for a growing community. Shane will advocate for practical, affordable solutions that make it easier for residents to get around.

🔍

Transparency & Accountability

Shane will hold the CVRD accountable with full transparency — asking the hard questions and demanding clear answers on behalf of every Area A resident.

🛠️

Small Business & Jobs

As an experienced entrepreneur, Shane will cut red tape to create opportunities for small businesses and ensure our kids have good local jobs here in Area A.

The Numbers Don't Lie

Taxes Are Out of Control

40% Property Tax Increase Under Current Director

Since the current director took office, average property taxes in Mill Bay have skyrocketed from roughly $3,950 in 2021 to nearly $6,000 in 2024. That's an unacceptable burden on Area A families.

Shane Ryan will bring the fiscal discipline and accountability needed to reverse this trend — fighting for every dollar of taxpayer money.

About Shane

Rooted in
Area A

Shane Ryan, 49, is a Malahat resident, small business owner, and former Alternate Director who has spent years fighting for the people of Area A. He is announcing his candidacy for Director in the fall 2025 election.

Shane, his wife Christine, and their 11-year-old daughter Elizabeth live entirely off-grid on the Malahat Mountain within Area A. Both Shane and Christine are self-employed and deeply committed to this community.

✦

Experienced entrepreneur — has built and sold successful businesses, career in private sector finance, and currently operates a successful software consulting company.

✦

Mill Bay Community League President for 5 years — fundraising for the local food bank, school lunch programs, and affordable housing.

✦

Active foster family — 30+ dogs rescued and fostered, parishioners of St. Francis Xavier Church, Mill Bay.

✦

Christine Ryan, volunteer charity auctioneer, has raised tens of thousands of dollars for many important local causes alongside daughter Elizabeth.

Family & Community

Invested in

Shane and Christine Ryan are more than residents — they're active contributors to the fabric of Mill Bay and Area A. From fostering rescue animals to volunteering at local schools and churches, the Ryan family is genuinely invested in this place they call home.
